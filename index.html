About Me

In West-Coast California, born and raised
On the computer is where I spent most of my days

I enjoy creating clean and simple designs with HTML/CSS/jQuery. My appreciation of great design stems from spending my youth immersed in print design. I grew up in a printing company my parents began years ago. My design experience begins from playing with Photoshop and Illustrator at an early age. When I began my undergrad I took a web design course and fell in love. Learning to appreciate beautiful design in the print world has now carried over into the digital realm of web design.

I am a really active person who loves the outdoors. I have been swimming for the better part of my life, competing since I was 9 years old. I swam for Cal Poly's NCAA D1 swim team for 3 years. On land I really like running, bouldering, hiking, and backpacking. I did my first triathlon in 2013 called Wildflower in Central California and hope to do more. Some other hobbies include: ceramics, photography, and soccer.
